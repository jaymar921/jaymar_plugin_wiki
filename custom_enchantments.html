Custom Enchantments

• A plugin that brings fun into the server, this plugin contains Custom Enchantments, Lucky Treasures, Custom Equipment Status, and more!!

• Turn your vanilla minecraft into next level! Plus it's a FREE plugin!

Note

• The developer is not responsible for any issues if you have a lot of plugins in your server causing plugin incompatibility.

• This plugin must not be used in huge servers, then if you do, take it as your own risk.

How do I enchant my stuff?

• Right click on the enchantment book in your main hand.

• Switch to your tool/weapon/armor that you want to enchant.

• Right click again while holding the item in your main hand.

• For enchanting tutorial, click here.(2.1.0 up)


Plugin Commands

• /storm_ally_list - Show the list of storm allies

• /storm_all {player} - Add a player to storm ally list

• /remove_storm_ally {player} - remove a player in storm ally list


• /customenchantment or /ce - give access to reload, give and remove

• /ce reload - reloads the config.yml

• /ce remove [enchantment] {player} - remove an enchantment from an item that the player holds

• /ce particles - enable/disable player particles

• /ce foodpocket - enable/disable foodpocket

• /ce item [class] - get a class of items that can only be found through lucky treasures

• /ce glow - glows a player when they have Custom Enchants

• /ce resethealth - resets all players health to 20.0 (2.1.4 and up)

• /enchantshop or /es - Opens the Shop GUI to buy custom enchantments




Helmet Enchantments

NIGHT VISION

Gain unlimited night vision effect when equipped

WATER BREATHING

Gain unlimited water breathing effect when equipped

HEAL

Slowly regenerate player's health overtime

BLIND

When attacked, there will be 20% chance for attacker to be blind for 3s

NULLED

When getting attacked on low health, the player will be invisible for 3s

(3 minutes cooldown)

MINER RADAR

When equipped, the player will be notified for nearby entities around 30 blocks radius, it can also detect ordinary and rare ores.

(Disable enchant if Scoreboard plugin is present)

Haste Aura

When equipped, the player will get haste effect. Nearby players around 5 blocks will also gain the effect.



Chestplate Enchantments

BLOCK

Block incoming attack if equipped. Up to 40% block chance.

REGAIN

If player's health drops below 3 hearts, the player will regenerate it's health back to full. If damage is greater than player health, the player dies

ABSORB

Absorbs incoming damage from attackers. The lower the player health, the higher resistance absorbed.

TANK

Gain extra health if equipped. Up to +20hp maximum health

EMNITY

Gain strength if equipped. The lower the player's health, the higher damage it deals.

OBSIDIAN PLATE

Has up to 30% chance of taking 5% of the attacker's weapon durability

CRAVING

When attacked, the enemy will be starved for a short amount of time. 20% chance to effect.

OMNIVAMP

There will be 25% chance to trigger OmniVamp from all damage types. OmniVamp, heals the player when attacked.

SATURATION

Slowly increase the player's food level overtime.

REGEN

Slowly increase the player's health overtime.

VANISH

The player will be invisible for 10s.

The player cannot attack, particles may be visible

(5 minutes cooldown)

Activate: /ce vanish



Leggings Enchantments

DEBUFF

Reduce a player's negative buff effect level. Applies to [Poison, Nausea and Hunger]

TIME TRAVEL

When the player takes damage, it will be teleported back to previous location 10 seconds before. Restoring it's HP and Hunger 10s before. 2 minutes cooldown

MOLTEN

Inflict the enemy in flame when attacked. Up to 18s of effect with 20% chance to trigger.

POISONOUS THORNS

Inflict the enemy with poison when attacked. 10% chance to trigger.

EMERGENCY DEFENSE

When the player is on very low health, it will deny all incoming damage for a short period of time. 3 minutes cooldown.

FREEZE

Inflict the attacker with slowness, the attacker cannot move for 2 seconds after a 5% chance of trigger.

FOOD POCKET

Every 2 minutes, the player will receive a random type of food. (Can be disabled by '/ce foodpocket')

ILLUSION

When attacking a player, you will be invisible by 3s. While invisible, you cannot attack the player and the player cannot see you. (particles can be seen)

COBWEB

When attacked, a cobweb will be placed on the attacker's location in result of slowing them.
